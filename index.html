About Us

Learn More About Our Movement

Feel Fine Trailblazers is more than a brand—it's a global movement designed to connect cyclists with adventure, discovery, and nature. Founded by Owen Ronald, a passionate cyclist and adventurer, Feel Fine Trailblazers was born out of a vision to inspire exploration and connection through cycling.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To inspire cyclists to embrace the joy of adventure, push their boundaries, and connect with nature.

Vision: To become the leading global community for cycling enthusiasts by combining exploration, sustainability, and well-being with innovative gear and unforgettable experiences.

Core Values:

  • Adventure: Encouraging exploration and new terrains.
  • Sustainability: Advocating eco-conscious choices and sustainable gear.
  • Innovation: Creating high-quality, purposeful gear for all terrains.
  • Community: Building meaningful connections with cyclists worldwide.

"Join the movement to explore new horizons, feel the freedom of cycling, and connect with a global community of trailblazers committed to adventure, sustainability, and shared experiences."

Have a question? Check out the FAQ

Explore the most common inquiries about our services, products, and commitment to sustainability.

What is Feel Fine Trailblazers' mission?

Our mission is to inspire cyclists to embrace adventure, push their boundaries, and connect with nature while fostering a supportive and like-minded community.

What types of products does your brand offer?

We offer a wide range of products including adventure bikes, cycling apparel, bikepacking gear, and essential cycling accessories designed for different terrains and environments.

Do you offer guided cycling tours and workshops?

Yes! We organize guided cycling tours, bikepacking weekends, and workshops catering to cyclists of all skill levels. These experiences are designed to enhance your skills and connect you with like-minded adventurers.

How does Feel Fine Trailblazers promote sustainability?

We are committed to sustainability by using eco-friendly materials, supporting trail conservation projects, and promoting recycling through bike repair programs and other community initiatives.

What community programs does Feel Fine Trailblazers offer?

We offer the Trailblazers Network—a space for cyclists to share routes, tips, experiences, and join events like adventure rides and community-building workshops.
